## üé® Special Features Demonstrated

### 1. Action/Reaction Scene Balance
- Scene 1: ACTION (pursuing goal under pressure)
- Scene 2: REACTION (processing consequences emotionally)
- Scene 3: ACTION (making critical decision)

This alternation creates natural pacing and rhythm.

### 2. Character Nicknames (AKA)
Shows how characters are known by different names:
- "Jax" (informal) vs "Captain Lee" (formal) vs "The Explorer" (reputation)
- Creates authenticity and depth

### 3. Location Alternate Names
Multiple cultural/historical names:
- "Aetheria" (Eternal name) vs "The Crystal World" (human descriptor) vs "Sanctuary Prime" (formal designation)

### 4. Storyline Tracking (sceneArcs)
Tracks which plots advance in each scene:
- "Main Plot" - primary story
- "Jaxon's Arc" - character development
- "Sophia's Arc" - subplot

Ensures no dropped plot threads!

### 5. Custom Rating Fields
Field1-4 used for scene analysis:
- Field1: Tension level (1-5)
- Field2: Pacing (1-5)
- Field3: Emotional depth (1-5)
- Field4: Action intensity (1-5)

Helps balance novel structure.

### 6. Narrative Modes (scMode)
- Dramatic Action (Scene 1)
- Dialogue (Scene 2, 3)
- Description, Exposition (other scenes)

Ensures variety in storytelling approach.
